10.3-2 ClosedSubsetOfProj
‣ ClosedSubsetOfProj( I )( operation )
‣ ClosedSubsetOfProj( mat )( operation )
‣ ClosedSubsetOfProj( str, R )( operation )

Returns: a CAP object

Construct a Zariski closed subset (as an object in the Zariski coframe of closed subsets a projective variety) from a morphism I=AsMorphismInCategoryOfRows( mat ) in the category of (graded) rows. The result is the projective support of the module-theoretic cokernel \(M\) of the morphism I in the associated Freyd category, i.e., the result is the projective vanishing locus of the (homogeneous) annihilator of \(M\).
